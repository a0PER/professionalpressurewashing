REVIEW THEMES AND INSIGHTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Most frequently praised attributes (across all 13 reviews):
  1. Responsiveness / same-day or next-day availability
  2. Punctuality (early arrival, honoring schedule)
  3. Fair / reasonable pricing
  4. Quality of results ("looks brand new," "above and beyond")
  5. Personal character (friendly, hardworking, trustworthy)
  6. Willingness to accommodate unusual requests (pre-paint prep,
     6 AM start, early scheduling for social events)

Job types mentioned in reviews:
  - House / home exterior washing
  - Self-storage buildings (interior and exterior)
  - Multiple commercial buildings (Tarik Issa: "many of our businesses")
  - Porch prep for painting (loose paint removal)
  - Driveway and sidewalks
  - Paved parking lot (mud/dirt removal -- new pavement)

Review timeline:
  Oldest:  7 years ago (Frankie McCartney, ~2018-2019)
  Newest:  2 months ago (Gabriel Castro and Ahmad Abdullah, ~April 2026)
  Active:  Business has received reviews as recently as April 2026,
           indicating Junior is still actively working despite the
           website being dormant since May 2018.

Owner reply pattern:
  Junior replies to roughly half the reviews. Every reply uses a
  consistent closing formula: "Pleasure doing business with you" or
  "It was a pleasure doing business with you." One reply adds
  "God bless and have a great day" -- consistent with the faith
  reference on the blog. One reply reveals context: "I hope your
  house sells quickly" (John Hujus review), showing he takes time
  to personalize responses.
